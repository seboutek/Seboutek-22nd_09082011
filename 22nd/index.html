Welcome to History Reenacted

Welcome to the official site for The 22nd North Carolina Infantry Regiment. An outstanding organization of Civil War Living Historians and Reenactors. We are devoted to telling the story of the Southron Soldier and preserving the Heritage and Honor of those on whose shoulders we now stand.


The 22nd North Carolina Field Command and Company A are based in historic Gettysburg, Pennsylvania with Companies B and K in North Carolina, Artillery Company I in West Virginia and Company H in Ohio. We invite you to explore our website. Your comments and questions are always welcome. Contact information is available on the Membership page.

“ N o nation rose so just and fair, None fell so pure of crime Worthy to have lived & known our gratitude Worthy to be hallowed & held In tender remembrance Worthy the fadeless fame which Confederate soldiers won Who gave themselves in life & death for us For the honor of North Carolina For the rights of the States For the liberties of the South For the principles of the Union, as these were handed down to them, By the fathers of our common Country.

Our Mission

To accurately and honorably portray the lives of the Confederate Soldiers in the 22nd North Carolina Infantry Regiment through community educational programs, live demonstations, and battle reenactments. Read Full Mission Statement
